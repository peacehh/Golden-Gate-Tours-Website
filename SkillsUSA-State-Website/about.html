ABOUT US

Golden Gate Tours (formerly known as Golden Gate Touring company) is a nationally recognized tourism company based in San Francisco, California. We provide top-notch immersive tours that span across the entirety of the Bay Area. We offer tours in the many beautiful natural parks, bustling cities, and vast seascapes. Our tour guides are extremely knowledgeable about the area and our tours are bound to leave you speechless in awe.

History of San Francisco and Golden Gate Tours:

1776 - San Francisco founded by Spanish colonists in search of Riches
1963 - Golden Gate Touring Company is founded by Charlee Ainslie in hopes of promoting tourism of the beautiful city he grew up in
1978 - Golden Gate Touring Company won the Leland Stanford Award for being the biggest contributor to tourism in the US.
1987 - Golden Gate Touring Company has a fleet of 100 tour boats
2012 - Golden Gate Touring Company rebrands to Golden Gate Tours

FAQ

What kind of tours are offered?

We offer a variety of tour packages, and you can mix and match packages at your personal request. Our basic packages are city tours, park tours, and boat tours.

How much do tours cost?

We strive to make sure you pay for what you need. Simply enter in a date, and our specially trademarked touring engine will find you the cheapest packages you will not find anywhere else. Base packages can start as low as $20!

Do you serve big groups?

Golden Gate tours believes that the bigger the group, the more enjoyable the tour. We serve groups of all sizes and even give exclusive discounts for larger groups.

Are there any hidden fees?

All fees are shown upfront, unlike most other touring companies. The price we offer you is the price you pay.
